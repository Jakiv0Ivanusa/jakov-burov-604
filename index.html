About me

I make art in the genre of psychedelic bio-horror. Fear does not close our eyes and ears with a saving veil of illusion: we hear the howl of cosmic chaos, we see monsters who rule over time and space, matter and energy, we feel a disastrous burden on our shoulders - a black double, which tirelessly prompts us to reject the banality of boring human days
